About me

I'm Quality Assurance Tester and Web App Tester from Metro Manila, Philippines, working in web development in manufacturing industry. I enjoy turning mediocre web applications into high-quality, beautiful and intuitive web application.
My job is to build and test your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive. Moreover, I add personal touch to your product and make sure that is eye-catching and easy to use. My aim is to bring across your message and identity in the most creative way. I helped develop web applications for many famous brand companies.
